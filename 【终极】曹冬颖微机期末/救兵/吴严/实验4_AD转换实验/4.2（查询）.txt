0809转换器查询方式接线记录
XD0-XD7接转换单元B0-B7
B0-B7的另外两行分别连接到8255和ad转换的D0-D7
剩下的一行前三位连接到abc选通
B8-B15连接到pa0-pa7 下面的插的b8连接到eoc
IOY0连接到8255cs
IOY1连接到ad cs 其他的按照书本


CODE SEGMENT
ASSUME CS:CODE
START:
MOV AL,90H
MOV DX,606H
OUT DX,AL  ;初始化8255 
XX:
MOV AL,00H
MOV DX,640H
OUT DX,AL

X1:
MOV DX,600H
IN AL,DX
TEST AL,80H
JNZ X1
X2: 
IN AL,DX
TEST AL,80H
JZ X2
MOV DX,640H
IN AL,DX
MOV DX,602H
OUT DX,AL
JMP XX
CODE ENDS
END START
